## Resources for Diverse Founders

Seattle's startup ecosystem includes programs specifically designed to support underrepresented founders. These resources provide mentorship, funding, and community tailored to your unique journey.

---

### BIPOC Founders

Programs supporting Black, Indigenous, and People of Color entrepreneurs in Greater Seattle.

**Key Resources:**
- **SEA619** - Seattle's premier accelerator for Black founders (October 2025 cohort)
- **Urban League of Metropolitan Seattle** - Business directory and technical assistance
- **Tacoma Urban League** - Coaching, workshops, credit support for Pierce County
- **Rainier Valley Community Development Fund** - Micro to commercial loans for SE Seattle
- **Founders First CDC** - Cohort programs for diverse founders
- **Business Impact NW** - Microloans and support for underserved entrepreneurs

**Certifications:**
- [OMWBE Certification](https://omwbe.wa.gov) - State certification for minority-owned businesses
- [King County Equity Now](https://kingcounty.gov) - BIPOC business programs

---

### Women Founders

Resources dedicated to women entrepreneurs across all five counties.

**Key Resources:**
- **WA Center for Women in Business** - Free coaching, Let's Talk Business webinars
- **Founders First CDC** - Women founder cohort programs
- **UW Women of Innovation** - Mentoring for women in the UW community
- **SCORE Women's Pitch Contest** - Annual competition with coaching and prizes
- **Economic Alliance Snohomish County** - Women business owner support

**Networks:**
- Women Who Startup Seattle
- Women in Cloud Seattle
- Female Founders Alliance (Seattle chapter)

---

### Veteran Founders

Programs honoring military service with business support.

**Key Resources:**
- **Washington DVA Veteran Business** - State certification and resources
- **EDB for Tacoma-Pierce County** - Veteran business support programs
- **Economic Alliance Snohomish County** - Veteran entrepreneur assistance
- **Enterprise for Equity** - Microloans prioritizing veterans
- **Bunker Labs Seattle** - Veteran entrepreneur network (monthly events)

**Federal Programs:**
- SBA Seattle District - 8(a), HUBZone certifications
- Washington APEX Accelerator - Government contracting support

---

### LGBTQ+ Founders

Supporting the LGBTQ+ entrepreneurial community.

**Key Resources:**
- **Pride Foundation** - Small grants for LGBTQ+ entrepreneurs
- **GSBA (Greater Seattle Business Association)** - LGBTQ+ chamber of commerce
- **StartOut Seattle** - LGBTQ+ founder networking and resources

**Inclusive Programs:**
Many accelerators and mentorship programs actively welcome LGBTQ+ founders:
- WTIA Founder Cohort
- Founders Network Seattle
- TiE Seattle

---

### Immigrant & Refugee Founders

Resources for New Americans building businesses.

**Key Resources:**
- **Craft3** - Lending prioritizing immigrant entrepreneurs
- **Business Impact NW** - Culturally competent business support
- **SCORE Seattle** - Multilingual mentoring available
- **Ventures** - Microloans and training for low-income entrepreneurs

**Language Support:**
- Many SBDC locations offer Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese counseling
- Seattle Public Library L2B program - Multilingual resources
