# Seattle

The Pacific Northwest's startup capital. Home to Amazon, Microsoft, Zillow, Redfin, and hundreds of venture-backed companies.

Seattle founders have access to 100+ free resources. No excuses.

## Why Seattle

- **$4.8B** venture capital invested in 2023
- **Top 10** U.S. startup ecosystem by volume
- **Zero state income tax** keeps more in your pocket
- **Tech talent density** second only to SF

## Where Founders Build

**South Lake Union** — Amazon HQ, biotech corridor, AI House Seattle at Pier 70

**Capitol Hill** — SURF Incubator, indie startups, creative tech

**University District** — UW CoMotion Labs, student founders, research spinoffs

**Pioneer Square** — Galvanize, Techstars alumni, co-working density

## Key Programs

- **AI House Seattle** — AI-focused workspace and community at Pier 70
- **SURF Incubator** — 8,000 sq ft for early-stage hardware and software
- **CoMotion Labs** — UW's startup accelerator for research commercialization
- **Techstars Seattle** — 13-week accelerator, $120K investment
- **Seattle Angel Conference** — 12-week competition, $150-200K prize
