# Lynnwood

South Snohomish County's commercial hub. Light rail extension arriving 2024 brings Seattle 25 minutes closer.

Lynnwood founders benefit from Snohomish County resources while staying connected to Seattle's ecosystem.

## Why Lynnwood

- **Light rail expansion** — direct connection to Seattle coming soon
- **Alderwood Mall area** — retail and commercial density
- **Snohomish County resources** at your doorstep
- **Lower costs** than King County

## Key Programs

- **Economic Alliance Snohomish County** — County-wide business support
- **Edmonds College SBDC** — Free business consulting nearby
- **Washington State Small Business Assistance** — State resources
