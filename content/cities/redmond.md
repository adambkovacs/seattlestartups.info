# Redmond

Microsoft's hometown. Nintendo of America. Tech giants and the talent pool they create.

Redmond founders operate in the shadow of tech giants with access to experienced engineers and enterprise connections.

## Why Redmond

- **Microsoft campus** — 50,000+ employees, spinoff culture
- **Tech talent density** — engineers, PMs, designers in your backyard
- **Eastside ecosystem** — Startup 425, GIX, Bellevue resources nearby
- **Trails and quality of life** — Marymoor Park, biking culture

## Key Programs

- **Startup 425 Accelerator** — Eastside-focused accelerator
- **Global Innovation Exchange (GIX)** — UW's tech and design program
- **Bellevue/Eastside SBDC** — Free business advising
- **DigiPen Institute** — Game development and tech talent
