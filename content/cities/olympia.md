# Olympia

Washington's capital. Government contracts, state agencies, and a growing entrepreneurial community.

Olympia founders have unique access to state procurement, policy influence, and public sector partnerships.

## Why Olympia

- **State capital** — government contract opportunities
- **Evergreen State College** — creative and social enterprise focus
- **Lower cost of living** than Seattle metro
- **GovTech opportunity** — proximity to state agencies

## Where Founders Build

**Downtown Olympia** — Center for Business and Innovation, Capitol Campus

**West Olympia** — St. Martin's University, small business corridor

**Lacey** — South Sound SBDC, commercial development

**Tumwater** — Historic breweries, manufacturing heritage

## Key Programs

- **Center for Business and Innovation** — Free advising, workshops, coworking
- **South Sound SBDC** — Free business consulting
- **Thurston Economic Development Council** — Grants and connections
- **City of Olympia Economic Development** — Permits, incentives, support
- **BEST Scholarships** — Lacey-Olympia-Tumwater business education
