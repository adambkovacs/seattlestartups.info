# Online & Statewide

Not in a major city? These resources work anywhere in Washington.

Virtual programs, state agencies, and online communities connect founders across the region.

## Why This Matters

- **Remote-first programs** — participate from anywhere
- **State-level resources** — available to all WA founders
- **Virtual mentorship** — SCORE, SBDCs offer remote advising
- **Online communities** — Slack groups, virtual events

## Key Programs

- **Washington SBDC Network** — 30+ locations, virtual consulting available
- **SCORE Puget Sound** — Free mentorship, virtual sessions
- **Washington State Commerce** — Statewide grants and programs
- **Choose Washington** — Business attraction and support
- **Export Voucher Program** — Help entering international markets

## State Agencies

- **Business.wa.gov** — Licensing, permits, compliance
- **Business License Wizard** — Find required licenses
- **Capital Access Program** — Loan guarantees statewide
