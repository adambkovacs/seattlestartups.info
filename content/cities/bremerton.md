# Bremerton

Kitsap County's largest city. Home to the Puget Sound Naval Shipyard and a growing maritime tech sector.

Bremerton founders can tap into defense contracts, maritime expertise, and a revitalizing downtown.

## Why Bremerton

- **Naval Shipyard** — Defense contracts and maritime talent
- **Fast ferry to Seattle** — 28 minutes to downtown
- **Lowest commercial rent** in the Greater Seattle region
- **Blue economy focus** — maritime and oceantech opportunities

## Where Founders Build

**Downtown Bremerton** — Waterfront revitalization, ferry terminal

**Silverdale** — Commercial center, Kitsap Mall area

**Naval Base Kitsap** — Defense adjacent opportunities

## Key Programs

- **Kitsap Economic Development Alliance** — Business support and connections
- **Olympic College SBDC** — Free business consulting
- **Bremerton Economic Development** — City incentives and permits
- **Contractor Coffee Hour** — Government contracting networking
