# Tacoma

Pierce County's largest city. 35 miles south of Seattle with half the rent.

Tacoma offers founders lower overhead, a tight-knit community, and direct access to the Port of Tacoma.

## Why Tacoma

- **50% lower** office costs vs. Seattle
- **Port access** for hardware, logistics, manufacturing
- **Growing tech scene** with UW Tacoma and PLU nearby
- **Diverse founder community** with strong BIPOC support networks

## Where Founders Build

**Downtown Tacoma** — Business Impact NW, city economic development programs

**Hilltop** — Spaceworks Tacoma, community-focused incubators

**Tacoma Dome District** — FabLab Tacoma, maker community

**Stadium District** — Coworking, networking events

## Key Programs

- **Spaceworks Tacoma** — Free workspace and mentorship for creative entrepreneurs
- **Business Impact NW** — Microloans and business coaching
- **FabLab Tacoma** — Maker space with CNC, laser cutters, 3D printers
- **253 Connections** — Pierce County networking and workshops
- **EDB Tacoma-Pierce** — Economic development grants and connections
