# Bellevue

The Eastside's commercial center. Microsoft, Meta, Amazon, and T-Mobile call it home.

Bellevue founders operate in one of the highest-income zip codes in the country with direct access to enterprise customers.

## Why Bellevue

- **Corporate HQ density** for B2B sales and partnerships
- **Global Innovation Exchange (GIX)** at UW Bothell
- **Startup 425** accelerator for Eastside companies
- **High-net-worth angel investors** in your backyard

## Where Founders Build

**Downtown Bellevue** — Corporate towers, enterprise sales proximity

**Crossroads** — Tech campuses, diverse founder community

**Factoria** — T-Mobile HQ, telecom-adjacent startups

**Bel-Red** — Spring District, mixed-use tech corridor

## Key Programs

- **Startup 425 Accelerator** — Eastside-focused program with mentorship
- **Bellevue College Innovation Lab** — Prototyping and student founder support
- **Eastside Entrepreneurs** — Monthly meetups and founder networking
- **Bellevue SBDC** — Free business advising and workshops
- **City of Bellevue Economic Development** — Grants and business support
