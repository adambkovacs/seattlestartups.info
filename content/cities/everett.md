# Everett

Snohomish County's hub. Boeing's largest facility. A manufacturing city building its tech future.

Everett founders benefit from aerospace talent, lower costs than Seattle, and strong county economic development support.

## Why Everett

- **Boeing presence** and aerospace supply chain
- **TheLab@Everett** — free coworking and prototyping
- **Manufacturing expertise** for hardware startups
- **40% lower** commercial rent vs. Seattle

## Where Founders Build

**Downtown Everett** — TheLab@Everett, city revitalization zone

**Paine Field** — Aerospace corridor, Boeing factory tours

**Everett Station** — Transit hub, mixed-use development

## Key Programs

- **TheLab@Everett** — Free workspace with 3D printers, CNC, laser cutters
- **Economic Alliance Snohomish County** — Business development resources
- **Everett SBDC** — Free business advising through Edmonds College
- **Invest Northwest SnoCo** — Angel investment network
