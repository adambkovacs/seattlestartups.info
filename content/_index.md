# Your Launchpad to the Seattle Startup Ecosystem

Discover **248+ verified free resources** for founders across King, Snohomish, Pierce, Kitsap, and Thurston counties. From accelerators to makerspaces, mentorship to funding—all in one place.

## Why This Exists

The Seattle startup community needed a single, reliable place to discover support resources. Information was fragmented across municipal, county, state, nonprofit, and university sources. This site changes that.

## What You'll Find

- **Accelerators & Incubators** — Free programs to help you grow
- **Mentorship & Advising** — SCORE, SBDC, and 24+ advisory networks
- **Funding Access** — Grants, competitions, and non-dilutive capital info
- **Spaces** — Makerspaces, coworking, kitchens, and labs
- **Community Events** — Meetups, pitch nights, and networking
- **Library Resources** — Premium databases and business consulting
- **University Programs** — Innovation hubs and student resources

## Our Standards

Every resource is:
- ✅ **Free to access** — No fees, no equity required
- ✅ **Verified** — Official URL with clear access path
- ✅ **Current** — Proof-of-life within 12 months
- ✅ **Local** — Serves Greater Seattle (5 counties)
