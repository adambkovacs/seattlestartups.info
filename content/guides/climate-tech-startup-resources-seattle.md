## Accelerators and Incubators

### Seattle Climate Innovation Hub (SCIH)

Launched January 2025 by Mayor Bruce Harrell, SCIH is the first downtown Seattle space dedicated to climate tech.

**Partners:**
- 9Zero
- VertueLab
- CoMotion Labs

**What's Available:**
- Dedicated climate tech workspace
- Connection to Seattle's cleantech ecosystem
- Access to partner programs
- Investor and mentor network

### [CoMotion Labs](/resources/comotion-labs/) Climate Tech Incubator

University of Washington's dedicated climate incubator operating from the Seattle Climate Innovation Hub.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Duration | 8 months |
| Focus | Climate and sustainability tech |
| Benefits | Tailored mentorship, professional network |
| Equity | None taken |
| First cohort | 2025 |

**What's Included:**
- Access to UW research and resources
- Climate tech professional network
- Mentorship from industry experts
- Sustainability-based pricing model

### [Cascadia CleanTech Accelerator](/resources/cascadia-cleantech-accelerator/)

A joint program from CleanTech Alliance and VertueLab running since 2016.

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Duration | 12-15 weeks (mid-May through mid-September) |
| Format | Virtual |
| Cost | $500 participation fee |
| Equity | None taken (non-dilutive) |
| Prize pool | $10,000 in cash prizes |

**Coverage Area:**
- Washington
- Oregon
- Western Canada
- Alaska
- Idaho

**Eligibility:**
- Raised less than $1M in funding
- Climate or clean technology focus

**Outcomes:**
- 50+ companies graduated since 2016
- Access to VertueLab and CleanTech Alliance networks

### Maritime Blue Innovation Accelerator

A 4-month program for maritime and ocean startups.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Duration | 4 months (accelerator) or 10 months (incubator) |
| Investment | $100K equity via SAFE |
| Office space | 2 years free at Blue Hub |
| Location | Fishermen's Terminal |

**Focus Areas:**
- Maritime innovation
- Clean water technology
- Alternative energy
- Ocean sustainability

**Recruitment Focus:**
- Women founders
- BIPOC founders
- Underserved communities

### VertueLab Programs

VertueLab runs multiple cleantech programs:

| Program | Focus |
|---------|-------|
| Cascadia CleanTech Accelerator | General cleantech |
| 45Camp | Deep decarbonization |
| Climate Impact Fund | Strategic investments |

**Investment Track Record:**
- $9.5 million invested to date
- Focus on high greenhouse gas reduction potential
- Philanthropic capital for strategic investments

### Fledge (Impact Accelerator)

A global impact accelerator based in Seattle.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Duration | 10 weeks |
| Format | Cohort-based curriculum |
| Model | Unique "repayable investment" |
| Focus | Impact and sustainability |

## Industry Organizations

### CleanTech Alliance

The Pacific Northwest's primary cleantech industry organization.

| Metric | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Member organizations | 1,100+ |
| Geographic reach | 17 U.S. states, 4 Canadian provinces |
| Founded | 2007 |

**Programs:**
- BUILT (decarbonizing infrastructure)
- Workforce Development
- Entrepreneurship
- Education
- Networking
- Awards
- Advocacy

**Lab2Launch:**
New resource connecting industry with academic institutions in the Northwest.

**Membership Benefits:**
- Business services and tools
- Lower-cost access to services
- Public policy advocacy
- Networking opportunities

**Special Offer (2026):**
New Standard members receive free CleanTech Day in Olympia ticket and $200 discount for eligible startups (invoice must be paid by January 22, 2026).

## How to Navigate These Resources

### Idea Stage (Pre-Revenue)

1. **Join CleanTech Alliance** for industry connections
2. **Attend CleanTech Innovation Showcase** to understand the ecosystem
3. **Apply to Cascadia CleanTech Accelerator** ($500 fee, no equity)
4. **Schedule SCORE mentorship** (free, request cleantech expertise)

### Prototype Stage

1. **Apply to CoMotion Labs Climate Tech Incubator** for 8-month program
2. **Connect with Seattle Climate Innovation Hub** for workspace
3. **Explore Maritime Blue** if focused on ocean/maritime tech
4. **Enter Cascadia CleanTech prize competition** for visibility

### Early Revenue

1. **Pitch to E8 Angels** for $250K-$2M seed funding
2. **Apply to VertueLab Climate Impact Fund** for strategic investment
3. **Join CleanTech Alliance** as a member for visibility
4. **Attend CleanTech Advocacy Day** to understand policy environment

### Growth Stage

1. **Connect with CEVG** for larger rounds
2. **Explore Maritime Blue** for office space and connections
3. **Apply for government cleantech programs** via APEX
4. **Scale with CleanTech Alliance** industry connections
