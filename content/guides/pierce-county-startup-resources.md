Pierce County has emerged as a serious alternative to Seattle, with **32 verified free programs** for entrepreneurs. This guide covers Tacoma, Lakewood, Puyallup, and surrounding cities in the South Sound.

Pierce County offers unique advantages for founders:

## Accelerators and Incubators

### [Pierce County Business Accelerator (PCBA)](/resources/pierce-county-business-accelerator-pcba/)

The primary accelerator for underserved entrepreneurs in Pierce County.

| Metric | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Businesses impacted | 570+ |
| Focus | BIPOC, veteran, and women-owned businesses |
| Programs | Workshop series, business growth training |

**Workshop Topics:**
- Business growth fundamentals
- Social media marketing
- Entrepreneurial mindset development

### William M. Factory Small Business Incubator

Operating since 1986, this is one of the longest-running incubators in Washington.

**Services:**
- Financial capital access
- Workshops and training
- Mentor and coach connections
- Networking opportunities

**BEE Project:**
Coordinates Pierce County's entire business-support ecosystem.

### [Spaceworks Tacoma](/resources/spaceworks-tacoma/)

A startup incubator, artscape hub, and co-working venue operating since 2010.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Founded | 2010 |
| Focus | First-time owners and creative professionals |
| Financing | Kiva 0% microloans available |

**Spaceworks Black Business Accelerator:**
Dedicated program advancing Black-owned businesses in Tacoma with business plan development and networking.

### [RAIN Incubator](/resources/rain-incubator/)

Medical and biotechnology incubator in downtown Tacoma.

| Feature | Details |
|---------|---------|
| Location | Downtown Tacoma |
| Focus | Medical and biotechnology |
| Mission | Generate sustainable, high-paying jobs |

### Tacoma Maritime Blue

Maritime business incubator focused on clean water and alternative energy.

**Key Benefits:**
- Free rent
- Mentorship access
- Funder connections
- Maritime industry network

**Focus Areas:**
- Clean water technology
- Alternative energy
- Maritime innovation
- Ocean sustainability

### SURGEtacoma

Co-working and resource hub serving the Tacoma entrepreneurial community.

**Who It Serves:**
- Startups
- Freelancers
- Students
- Nonprofits

### Startup253

Resource and networking hub connecting entrepreneurial leaders with venture capital.

### VIBE at UW Tacoma

Veterans Incubator for Better Entrepreneurship at University of Washington Tacoma.

**Focus:**
- Veteran entrepreneurs
- Modern startup lifecycle training
- Applied business development

## Funding in Pierce County

### City of Tacoma Loan Programs

| Program | Amount | Eligibility |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| Uplift Loan Program | Up to $100,000 | 3+ years in operation |
| Accelerator Loan Program | Over $500,000 | 5+ years in operation |
| Small business loans | Varies | City businesses |
| Microloans | Varies | Early-stage |
| Historic rehabilitation loans | Varies | Historic buildings |
| Fire safety loans | Varies | Compliance needs |

**Uplift Loan Details:**
- Collateral-free loans
- Up to $100,000
- Requires 3+ years of operation
- Tacoma-based businesses

## How to Navigate Pierce County Resources

### Idea Stage

1. **Visit Make It Tacoma** for resource overview
2. **Attend PCBA workshop** on entrepreneurial mindset
3. **Schedule SCORE mentorship** (free)
4. **Join SURGEtacoma** for community

### Early Stage (0-2 years)

1. **Apply to Spaceworks** for incubator support
2. **Explore Kiva microloans** (0% interest)
3. **Connect with William M. Factory** for mentorship
4. **Apply to PCBA** if BIPOC, veteran, or woman-owned

### Growth Stage (3+ years)

1. **Apply to Uplift Loan Program** (up to $100K)
2. **Connect with EDB** for expansion support
3. **Explore C-PACER** for efficiency upgrades
4. **Scale with NDC Grow America** funding

### Specialized Tracks

**Veterans:**
- VIBE at UW Tacoma
- PCBA veteran track
- VBOC at Business Impact NW

**Maritime/Cleantech:**
- Tacoma Maritime Blue (free rent)
- Maritime Blue One Ocean
- Port of Tacoma connections

**Medical/Biotech:**
- RAIN Incubator
- UW Tacoma research partnerships
